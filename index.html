Where To Go?

Instructions:

  • Type in the address of interest and click "Lookup" to set the originating point.

  • Put in the search term, ie: music, park etc and hit "Find" to locate matched establishments in the vicinity.

  • Click on the red markers on the map for a popup with some infomation of the selected locale.

  • While the popup is open, click the "Direction" button for driving directions from the point of origination to the marker location.
